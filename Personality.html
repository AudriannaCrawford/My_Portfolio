Impact of My Personality

I tend to be a person who gets so frustrated when things don't go as planned (for example the crash that happened with my laptop deleting my assignment which I now have to redo). I tend to need a serious crying season, 2 or 3 sessions at that. Before I can push forward, however, this has helped me to keep trying even if I fail at least I tried. I'd say I'm a very emotional person with an overactive mind, which enabled me to go about finding creative ways to calm these emotions. A hobby of mine is doing nails or art which allows me to calm my self quiet my mind and help me to focus on one thing at a time.
